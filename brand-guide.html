01 — Brand Overview
Who we are.

Studio2J is a personal shopping service based in Seoul and Tokyo. We buy Korean and Japanese goods on behalf of customers worldwide — from region-locked online shops to goods available only at illustration and stationery fairs in person.

We are not a corporate reseller. We are two people, physically on the ground, attending fairs and browsing shops so our customers don't have to. Every order is handled personally. Every parcel leaves with a handwritten note.

Positioning: Proxy buying service with fair hauls as proof of presence. The fair tracker exists to show we are actually there — not just another online middleman.

06 — Voice & Tone
Personal, not corporate.

Studio2J's voice is warm, precise, and quietly confident. We are two people who love stationery and illustration and happen to live in Seoul and Tokyo. We write like that person, not like a logistics company.

Do
 Direct and warm — "We'll handle the rest"
 Specific — "Korea Post EMS, with tracking"
 Human — "Every parcel leaves with a handwritten note"
 Confident without boasting — "Care, not corporate"
 Use em-dashes — not hyphens or ellipsis
Don't
 Corporate jargon — "leverage", "seamless", "solution"
 Over-promise — "guaranteed", "100% safe"
 Casual to the point of unprofessional — slang
 Rewrite copy without approval — every word is tuned
 Exclamation marks in formal contexts
Key phrases (use exactly)
"Korean and Japanese shops, delivered."
"We're your person on the ground."
"We're physically there, every month."
"From Seoul and Tokyo, with care"
07 — Rules at a Glance
The non-negotiables.
1
Never use a logo image. The wordmark is always set in Fraunces typography.
2
Only use the 8 palette colors. No pure white, no pure black, no additions.
3
Homepage copy is fixed. Do not rewrite or "improve" any headline or body copy without approval.
4
Border weight: 0.5px everywhere. Hairlines, not visible strokes.
5
Italic emphasis = dark blue. Whenever Fraunces italic is used for emphasis in a heading, the color is always #1F3A5F.
